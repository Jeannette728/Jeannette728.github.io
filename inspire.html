Princess Diana

Why did so many love Princess Diana? Diana was an embodiment of love, she desperately loved her children and she also loved her public. She stood up for what she believed in no matter the consequences.

What made her so special?

  • She was courageous in using her celebrity status to draw awareness to serious matters of the world
  • Her charm attracted the public to her
  • She changed the world by spreading kindness
  • She was very involved in charity work
  • Her legacy is an inspiration

Princess Diana had a natural empathy for everyone. She was a smart, kind and giving person who genuinley cared about her people.
